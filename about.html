Our Leadership Team

Insoo Kim

Insoo Kim is the President and Founder of ALKOR Semicon Total Solutions Inc. He graduated from Hanyang University in 1991 with a B.S. in Metallurgical Engineering.

In 2002, Kim joined Samsung Electronics in South Korea as the Purchasing Manager of Semiconductor Raw Materials, later relocating to Samsung Austin Semiconductor to become the Purchasing Sr. Manager. He also has three years of experience as the Lead Sourcing Manager of Fab8 at GlobalFoundries in Malta, New York.

In 2013, Insoo Kim started ALKOR Semicon Total Solutions Inc. and has been connecting semiconductor companies in both the United States and South Korea for over a decade.

Heeran Kim

Heeran Kim is the CFO of ALKOR Semicon Total Solutions Inc. She received her B.S. in Chemistry from Sungsim Women's University in 1995.

In 1995, Kim joined Samsung Electronics in South Korea and worked as a Process Engineer for five years. She joined ALKOR Semicon Total Solutions Inc. in 2013, bringing her diverse qualifications and skills to the company.
