Connecting the East and West Coast

ALKOR is a distributor of Korean products that are used for semiconductor manufacturing. Korean chip makers, such as Samsung and SK Hynix, have grown with their domestic suppliers aligned on their leading-edge technology. The items of Korean suppliers include but are not limited to, spare parts, raw materials, sub-fab equipment, and repairs. ALKOR brings the value of Korean supplies to the U.S. market connecting two different cultures.
